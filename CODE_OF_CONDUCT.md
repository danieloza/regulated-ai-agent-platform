# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project welcomes constructive participation from people with different
backgrounds and levels of experience. Contributors, maintainers, and reviewers
are expected to:

- communicate respectfully and focus criticism on the work;
- make technical claims with evidence and distinguish facts from assumptions;
- accept review in good faith and help others improve their contributions;
- respect privacy, confidentiality, and responsible-disclosure boundaries;
- avoid harassment, discrimination, intimidation, personal attacks, and
  disruptive behavior.

These expectations apply in issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and
other spaces used to represent the project.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions that violate this standard
and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary.
Enforcement decisions should consider context, severity, repeated behavior, and
the safety of affected participants.

Report conduct concerns privately through the maintainer's
[GitHub profile](https://github.com/danieloza). Do not include sensitive personal
information in a public issue. Security vulnerabilities must follow
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

Maintainers will handle reports as confidentially as reasonably possible and
will disclose information only when needed to assess or address the concern.
